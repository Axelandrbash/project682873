Traditional methods of working with communities (lectures, seminars, trainings) have stopped working and will never be effective. This is recognized by most professional community development experts, activation and involvement of the population, non-formal education for young people and adults.

Gamification is a soft power that quietly modifies behavior patterns for participants, ensures quick assimilation of new knowledge and motivates the introduction of change.

Within 3 days in the format of the author's workshop you will learn about effective game tools, learn how to use them and plan your further effective work with communities.
The Goal:
to introduce gamification approach to participants as an effective method in community development work and adult education.

Number of participants: 10-12

Price: from $6000 (for 12 participants without logistics)

Target audience: trainers, facilitators, experts on community development, community managers
Expected results
participants know fundamental principles and concepts of gamification

participants know how World of Communities board game can be used in different areas of community development (community engagement, adult education, youth education, financial literacy)2

participants diagnosed issues that decrease the efficiency of their work in community

participants generated ideas how World of Communities gamification principles can be applied in their work
