What's the point of World of Communities: [online]?
or how everything useful and educational is embedded in the game
Local identity
The game reality corresponds to the map of Ukraine, on which each new player chooses the community in which he or she will "live" and which he or she will develop online.
1
Society and politics
Players can participate in the implementation of local policies (infrastructure, educational projects), national policies (requires the interaction of leaders from different communities) and respond to challenges from the "outside world" that will periodically generate crises and opportunities that are independent of players' will.
2
Cooperation and leadership
To gain access to additional game tools, "residents" of the community need to engage their fellow citizens and organize joint activities with other players.
3
ONLY 3 - 5% OF THE TOTAL COMMUNITY RESIDENTS NUMBER PARTICIPATE WORLD OF COMMUNITIES ONLINE IS ENOUGH TO START IMPLEMENTING THE REAL CHANGE OFFLINE.
Finance and participation
It's easy to complain about bad road conditions until you understand how local budget is formed, and most importantly, how to influence it. Thanks to the simulator, players are able to manage national and local budgets through key participation tools.
4
Stimulating local business
To understand the role of local businesses in the game, there is a tax system and an opportunity for players to start their own business. Community fund in the game will show the importance of local philanthropy in real life.
5
Real impact
In the online game, participants receive homework to accomplish in real life (for example, to find out about the local budget), and have access to a shared e-library of local development projects open to all players.
